Short-form video automation

Turn theme-park news into short videos — automatically.

MagicCast Studio helps creators produce daily short-form videos about Disney theme-park news and publish them straight to their own YouTube and TikTok channels, end to end.

📰

Sources the news

Pulls the day's Disney parks stories from trusted news feeds and picks the most worthwhile ones.

✍️

Writes & narrates

Drafts a tight script and a natural voiceover, then composes a vertical 9:16 video with captions.

🚀

Publishes for you

Uploads the finished video to your own connected accounts — YouTube and TikTok — on your schedule.

📈

Learns what works

Reads your own watch-through and view data to help decide what to make next.

How it works

  1. Connect your channels. Authorize MagicCast to post to your own YouTube and TikTok accounts.
  2. It builds the video. Script, voiceover, visuals, and captions are assembled into a vertical short.
  3. You review or automate. Approve each one, or let it publish on a schedule you set.
  4. It improves over time. Your own performance stats guide future picks.

How MagicCast uses TikTok

With your explicit authorization via TikTok Login Kit, MagicCast Studio uses the Content Posting API to upload videos you create to your own TikTok account. It requests only the permissions it needs: video.upload and video.publish to publish your videos, and user.info.basic to identify the connected account.

MagicCast acts solely on the account of the person who connects it. It does not access, collect, or post on behalf of any other users, and it stores its authorization token locally on the operator's own computer. You can disconnect at any time from within the app or your TikTok settings. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
